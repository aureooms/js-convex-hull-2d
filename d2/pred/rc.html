Ray casting algorithm.

Counts the number of times a ray intersects a polygon. If the count is a multiple of 2 then the point lies outside the polygon, inside otherwise.

Hypothesis :

  • polygon is represented as an array of vertices
  • polygon has at least 2 vertices

Parameters:

  • polygon must be an array.
    (array of vertices)

  • i must be an integer.
    (index of the first vertex of the polygon)

  • j must be an integer.
    (index past the last vertex of the polygon)

  • p must be a point.
    (origin point of the ray)

  • q must be a point.
    (direction point of the ray see http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Point_in_polygon)
